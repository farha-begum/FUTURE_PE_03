# AI SEO Blog & Content Cluster Generator

## Purpose
Generate SEO-optimized blog content for business websites.

## Input Required
- Business Type
- Target Audience
- Main Service
- Target City
- Primary Keyword
- Tone of Voice

## Tasks
1. Generate SEO blog titles
2. Create H1-H3 blog outline
3. Write long-form blog content
4. Generate FAQs
5. Create internal linking ideas
6. Suggest content clusters
7. Optimize for search intent
8. Include CTA sections

## Output Requirements
- SEO optimized
- Human readable
- Business-focused
- High engagement
- Search intent aligned

## Example Prompt

Create a long-form SEO blog for:
- Business: Digital Marketing Agency
- City: Hyderabad
- Keyword: Best SEO services in Hyderabad
- Tone: Professional

Include:
- H1-H3 headings
- FAQs
- CTA
- Internal linking suggestions
- Meta description
