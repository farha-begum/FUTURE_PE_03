
# 🚀 AI SEO Content Cluster System

An advanced AI-powered prompt engineering system designed to generate SEO-optimized blog content, topical authority clusters, and local SEO strategies for business websites.

---

# 📌 Project Overview

This project demonstrates how Large Language Models (LLMs) can be used to automate modern SEO content workflows using structured prompt engineering.

The system generates:

- Long-form SEO blogs
- H1–H3 content structures
- Topic clusters
- Semantic keyword mapping
- Local SEO pages
- Internal linking strategies
- Meta titles & descriptions
- Search intent optimized content

---

# 🧠 Core Objective

Build an AI-driven content workflow capable of helping businesses:

- Improve organic visibility
- Rank for commercial keywords
- Build topical authority
- Increase inbound traffic
- Generate qualified leads

---

# ⚙️ AI Prompt Engineering Workflow

## Step 1 — Business Input Collection

The system collects:

- Business type
- Target audience
- Main service
- Location/city
- Primary keyword
- Tone of voice

---

## Step 2 — SEO Intent Mapping

The AI identifies:

- Informational intent
- Commercial intent
- Transactional intent
- Navigational intent

---

## Step 3 — Content Structure Generation

The system creates:

- H1 headlines
- H2 sections
- H3 subsections
- FAQ schema ideas
- CTA sections

---

## Step 4 — Topical Authority Clustering

AI generates:

- Pillar content ideas
- Supporting blogs
- Internal linking structure
- Semantic relevance mapping

---

## Step 5 — Local SEO Optimization

The workflow adapts content for:

- City-specific targeting
- Local business visibility
- Google Business relevance
- Local keyword placement
